ACKNOWLEDGMENTS
We wish we could say all the designs in this book are original. The
truth is that most of the ideas discussed here can also be found
elsewhere; in engineering blogs, research papers, code, youtube
presentations, and other places. We have collected these elegant
ideas and considered them, then added our personal experiences,
to present them here in an easy-to-understand way. Additionally,
this book has been written with the significant input and reviews of
more than a dozen engineers and managers, some of whom made
