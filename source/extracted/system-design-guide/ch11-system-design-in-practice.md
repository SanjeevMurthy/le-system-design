Part 3: System Design in Practice
In this Part, we transition from theoretical concepts to practical application, exploring how the
principles and components we’ve discussed are used to design real-world systems. This section
is designed to bridge the gap between theory and practice, providing you with hands-on
experience in tackling complex system design challenges.
By the end of this Part, you'll have a robust toolkit for tackling any system design challenge,
whether in a professional setting or an interview scenario.
